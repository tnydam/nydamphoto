About the Studio

Honest Photography for the Moments That Last

We're Arlen and Tanya Nydam — a husband-and-wife studio in Austin, telling the stories that matter most, the way they actually happened.

Our Story

Nydam Photography is an Austin, Texas studio with eighteen years of editorial, photojournalistic, and cinematic photography. We specialize in weddings, senior portraits, families, engagements, Quinceañeras, sacraments, corporate portraits, sports, and events — bringing refined, controlled lighting and intentional direction to every session.

We believe every story is unique, and deserves to be told with honesty and heart.

What We Stand For

Genuine. Intentional. Timeless.

Genuine

Real moments over staged poses. We're present for your day — never in the way of it.

Intentional

Timeless

The People Behind the Camera

Meet the Team

We are a studio of storytellers, here to capture the heart of your moment — not to direct it or stage it, but to be present for it. From candid smiles to grand celebrations, every detail is preserved with care.

Arlen Nydam

Lead Photographer · Founder

Arlen earned his PhD in English and started shooting professionally at the same time — they were never separate pursuits. He reads a room the way a close reader reads a page: looking for what's true, what's fleeting, what will still matter twenty years from now. The one your guests will never notice, until they see the photos.

Tanya Nydam

Co-Founder · COO · Second Photographer

Tanya runs the operations and strategy that make every session seamless, from your first inquiry to your final gallery. On wedding days she steps behind the camera, bringing a second perspective that captures what one photographer can't be in two places to catch. The reason everything works the way it should.
