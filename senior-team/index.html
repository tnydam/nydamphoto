Every Senior Year Has a Story

This is how we tell it.

Senior year isn't remembered in one photograph.

It's remembered in hundreds of little moments. Walking into school for the last first day. One more Friday night under the stadium lights. Trying on a prom dress. College acceptance letters spread across the kitchen table. Laughing with the friends who have been beside you since elementary school. Realizing — almost without noticing — that childhood is quietly becoming memory.

That's the story we photograph. Not just the portraits. The year.

Every Story Begins Somewhere

Meet Brynley

Chapter One

Finding Their People

Before a senior ever steps in front of a camera alone, they get to do it alongside other seniors — at a holiday shoot, a spring session, whatever the season brings. Some of these seniors didn't know each other before this year. By the end of it, they're laughing in the background of each other's photos.

Chapter Two

Becoming Comfortable

The first time anyone's in front of a camera, it shows — the stiff shoulders, the unsure smile. By the third or fourth time, that's gone. We've watched seniors arrive nervous in the fall and leave their final session not even thinking about the camera anymore, because by then, they've forgotten to be self-conscious.

Chapter Three

The Portrait They'll Remember

This is the one your family will hang on the wall. Fully styled — hair, makeup, the works — and built entirely around who your senior actually is. Editorial, classic, athletic, theatrical, quiet, loud: whatever feels like them, this is where it gets made.

Chapter Four

Looking Back Together

We sit down together and go through the finished images in person — not a link in an inbox, but a real moment where you watch your family's reaction to seeing this season for the first time, all at once.

Questions

NP Seniors FAQ

How many seniors are accepted into NP Seniors each year?
We intentionally welcome only a small number of seniors into the program each year, so every family receives the time, guidance, and attention that define the experience.
Can I book a single senior session instead?
Yes. Individual senior sessions are available separately through our Senior Collections, for families who'd prefer one session rather than the full NP Seniors experience.
What if I'm nervous about being photographed?
The seasonal portrait moments give you real time in front of the camera throughout the year, with guidance every step, so your signature session never feels like the first time you've been photographed.
Do I need to bring my own hair and makeup artist?
No. Professional styling, hair, and makeup are included for your signature senior session.
When should I reach out?
As early as possible. Seniors in the program get priority planning and calendar access before the public calendar opens, so earlier inquiries have the best chance at being included.
Are the seasonal moments the same for every group?
No. Each group's seasonal moments are planned around that group's own season and timeline — they evolve naturally rather than following a fixed, identical calendar.
How do I see my images after the signature session?
After your signature session, we walk through your gallery together and help you choose images to print, gift, or frame.
What's included in the experience?
Seasonal portrait moments with your group, a fully styled signature senior session, planning support, posing guidance, gallery delivery, and access to Portrait Credits through referrals.
What are Portrait Credits?
Portrait Credits are earned when a friend you refer completes their own paid session. Credits don't expire for 12 months and can be used toward prints, canvas, albums, or bonus sessions depending on how many you've earned.
