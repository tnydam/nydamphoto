First Communion Mini Sessions

Portrait sessions made for this sacred milestone.

First Communion is one of the most meaningful moments in a child's faith journey. These mini sessions are designed specifically for this day — unhurried, beautifully lit, and guided so your child feels relaxed and confident in front of the camera.

Arlen photographs inside the church and in the surrounding outdoor spaces, giving you a variety of settings in one short session. The result is a gallery of images that truly captures who your child is on this day.
