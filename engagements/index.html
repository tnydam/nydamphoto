Engagement Sessions

Austin Engagement Photography

The session before the wedding — where the two of you get comfortable in front of the camera, and we learn how you move together, long before the day itself.

View the Portfolio Explore Wedding Collections

An engagement session is rarely about the photographs alone. It's the first time we work together — a relaxed hour or two that makes your wedding day feel familiar instead of new. By the time we photograph your wedding, the camera is no longer a stranger, and neither are we.

Why It's Worth It

What an Engagement Session Gives You

01

Practice before the day

You learn what to do with your hands and where to look; we learn how you connect. Nothing about the wedding day feels like the first time.

02

Photographs you'll actually use

Save-the-dates, invitations, the guest book, and the first framed print of the two of you together.

03

A relationship, not a transaction

Your wedding portraits look the way they do because we were comfortable with one another first.

Our Approach

Directed Only When It Helps

We photograph engagement sessions the way we photograph weddings — mostly unobtrusive, stepping in to guide only when it earns its place. We'll suggest somewhere that means something to the two of you, or a spot around Austin or the Hill Country with room to move, and then largely let you be.

The direction we give is simple and physical: walk here, stay close, look at each other. The moments in between are the ones we keep. You can read more about how we work on Our Approach.
