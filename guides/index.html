Why These Guides Exist

Prepared Clients Photograph Better

These guides come from real questions real clients have asked us, session after session — not a generic checklist, but the things that actually come up before a wedding, a senior session, or a branding shoot.

Knowing what to expect reduces stress around wardrobe, posing, timing, and what the day or session will actually feel like. Whether you're ready to book now or still researching, these resources are here to help — and they're part of the same guided, intentional experience you'll have working with us in person.
