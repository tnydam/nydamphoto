Why These Guides Exist

Prepared Seniors Photograph Better

These guides answer the questions families ask most often — about wardrobe, posing, makeup, expectations, and confidence. Seniors don't need to know how to pose, and parents don't need to figure everything out alone.

The goal is to make the session feel calm, guided, and personal, long before anyone steps in front of the camera.

Questions

Senior Guide FAQ

Which senior guide should we start with?
Start with How to Prepare for Senior Photos for the big picture, then What to Wear for Senior Pictures once you're ready to plan outfits.
When should we book senior portraits?
Most families book in spring or summer of junior year through early senior year, which gives the most flexibility on dates and locations.
What should my senior wear?
Our wardrobe guide covers colors and outfit ideas in detail, but generally a mix of a few outfits that feel like your senior's everyday style works best.
Do you help with posing?
Yes. We guide posture, movement, and expression throughout the session, so your senior is never left wondering what to do.
Can we include sports, dance, music, or hobbies?
Yes, and we encourage it. A senior's interests can be woven into the session without making it feel like a theme shoot.
What is NP Seniors?
NP Seniors is a separate, limited program — built for seniors who want a more connected experience, with seasonal sessions, featured portraits, and early access.
Are these guides only for booked clients?
No. These are complimentary resources for anyone planning senior portraits, whether you've booked with us or are still researching.
Do you offer senior portraits for guys?
Yes. Our Senior Style Guide for Guys is built specifically around looks and posing that feel natural, not forced.
Where do we see senior collections?
Senior Collections outlines our Essential, Signature, and All-Inclusive senior portrait sessions, including pricing and what's included.
