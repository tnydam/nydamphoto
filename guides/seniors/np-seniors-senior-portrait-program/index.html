What Is NP Seniors?

NP Seniors is a limited senior portrait program in Austin for seniors who want priority access, more creative opportunities, and a more connected experience throughout senior year. Seniors in the program get priority access to book their signature session before the public calendar opens, plus two to three seasonal editorial experiences planned around their group's own timeline. It isn't a bundle of extra sessions — it's priority access and a guided experience built around one personalized signature session.

What NP Seniors Is

NP Seniors is not four sessions for one price — it's an exclusive senior year experience for a limited group of seniors. The value isn't in how many times you're photographed; it's in the seasonal editorial experiences that come with being part of a small group, and priority access to book your signature session ahead of the public calendar.

A regular senior session is one complete appointment, booked on the public calendar like any other booking. NP Seniors is structured differently — seniors in the program get first access before the calendar opens publicly, plus the kind of guided, connected experience that a single appointment doesn't allow for.

Seasonal Editorial Experiences

Every NP Seniors group includes two to three exclusive seasonal editorial experiences, planned around that group's own timeline, the time of year, and what fits creatively. Depending on a group's timeline, these may include holiday portraits, fall editorial portraits, spring portraits, cap-and-gown portraits, best-friend portraits, or mom-and-senior portraits — themes vary by group and aren't identical or guaranteed across every group.

These aren't generic mini sessions. They're editorial group experiences that help seniors get comfortable in front of the camera, build variety into senior year, and create images beyond the traditional senior portrait session — all well before the signature session itself.

Priority Access & Your Signature Session

The heart of NP Seniors is your signature senior portrait session. Seniors in the program receive priority access to book that session before dates open publicly — the best dates go to the program first. As with any session at Nydam Photography, you choose the format, style, and location for your signature session; what NP Seniors adds is the chance to lock in your preferred date before the public calendar opens.

Quick check: priority access is about timing, not creative direction — every client at Nydam Photography chooses their own format, style, and location. NP Seniors gets you first access to the calendar so you're not competing for dates once it opens publicly.

What's Included

  • Priority access to the senior portrait calendar
  • A personalized signature senior session
  • Two to three seasonal editorial experiences with your group
  • Professional stylist, hair, and makeup for your signature session
  • Grooming for guys
  • Planning support before your signature session
  • Posing guidance throughout
  • Full digital gallery delivery
  • In-person Gallery Reveal
  • Access to Portrait Credits through referrals

Portrait Credits

Portrait Credits are earned when a friend you refer completes their own paid session. There's no limit on how many you can earn, and credits are valid for 12 months from when they're earned. Portrait Credits are a separate benefit from the program itself — they're earned through referrals, not included automatically.

What to Expect

The path through NP Seniors generally looks like this: a planning conversation to understand your senior's personality and style, two to three seasonal editorial experiences across the season planned around your group's timeline, priority booking of your signature session ahead of the public calendar, and finally an in-person Gallery Reveal where you see your finished images together and choose what to print, gift, or frame.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many spots are available in NP Seniors?
NP Seniors is intentionally limited to a small group each year, so reaching out early gives you the best chance at being included.
Can I book just one session instead of the full program?
Yes. If you're looking for a single senior portrait session rather than priority access and seasonal editorial experiences, individual senior collections are available separately.
What does priority access actually mean?
Priority access means seniors in the program can book their signature session date before the public calendar opens, so the best dates and times are available to them first.
Do I need to bring my own hair and makeup artist?
No. Professional styling, hair, and makeup are included for your signature session.
Are the seasonal editorial experiences the same for every group?
No. Each group's seasonal editorial experiences are planned around that group's own timeline, the season, and what fits creatively -- they're not a fixed, identical calendar for every senior.
What is included in NP Seniors?
Priority calendar access, a personalized signature senior session, two to three seasonal editorial experiences with your group, planning support, posing guidance, and gallery delivery.
What are Portrait Credits?
Portrait Credits are earned when a friend you refer completes their own paid session. They don't expire for 12 months and can be used toward prints, canvas, albums, or bonus sessions depending on how many you've earned.
What's the difference between NP Seniors and a regular senior session?
A regular senior session is one complete appointment booked on the public calendar. NP Seniors adds seasonal editorial experiences with your group and priority calendar access, so your signature session is booked before the calendar opens to the public.
