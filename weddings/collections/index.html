Questions

Questions About Collections

How much coverage do we need?
It depends on your day. Essential suits intimate weddings and elopements, Signature fits a full traditional wedding day, and Wedding Story is built for multi-location celebrations and wedding weekends. We're happy to talk through your specific plans.
Do you require a retainer?
Yes. A 30% retainer secures your date and collection, with the remaining balance due before the wedding.
Can we customize a collection?
Yes. Each collection is a starting point, and we can adjust coverage, add a second photographer to Essential, or include additional events to fit your day.
Is travel included?
Travel within Central Texas is included. Destination weddings and travel beyond the area are quoted individually.
What if our timeline changes?
Wedding timelines shift, and we plan for that. We'll work with you as details firm up so your coverage still fits the day.
When do we receive our gallery?
Wedding galleries are typically delivered within six to eight weeks, with a handful of favorites shared sooner.
