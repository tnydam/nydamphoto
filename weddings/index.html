Questions

Wedding Photography, Frequently Asked

How far in advance should we inquire?
Most couples reach out six to eighteen months before their wedding, since dates on popular weekends fill first. If your wedding is sooner, it's still worth checking availability.
Do you require a retainer?
Yes. A 30% retainer reserves your date and collection, with the remaining balance due before the wedding.
Can we book shorter coverage instead of a full day?
Yes. Our Essential collection covers the core hours of the day, while Signature and Wedding Story extend into full-day storytelling from getting ready through reception.
Is an engagement session included?
A complimentary engagement session is included with our Signature and Wedding Story collections, and can be added to Essential.
Do you provide a second photographer?
Yes. A second photographer is included with our Signature and Wedding Story collections, and can be added to Essential. This covers separate getting-ready spaces, the ceremony, and the reception all at once.
Do you photograph Catholic weddings?
Yes, including full Nuptial Mass ceremonies, with care for the liturgy alongside our editorial wedding-day coverage.
How long until we receive our wedding gallery?
Wedding galleries are typically delivered within six to eight weeks, with a handful of favorites shared sooner.
Do you offer albums and prints?
Yes. Heirloom albums and fine-art prints are available directly from your gallery, printed by professional labs.
Do you travel for weddings outside Austin?
Yes. We photograph throughout Central Texas and the Hill Country, and we're available for destination weddings as well.
