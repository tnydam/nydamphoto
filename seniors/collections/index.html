Questions

Questions About Senior Collections

Which senior portrait collection should we choose?
It depends on how much variety your senior wants. Essential suits a focused, classic session. Signature fits seniors who want more outfits, locations, or personality woven in. All-Inclusive is for a fully styled, complete experience.
When should we book senior portraits?
Most families book in spring or summer of junior year through early senior year, which gives the most flexibility on dates and locations.
Do you help with posing?
Yes. Every collection includes guided posing and direction, so your senior is never left wondering what to do.
How many outfits should my senior bring?
We typically recommend 2–3 outfits for any session. Signature includes multiple outfit changes, and All-Inclusive includes unlimited outfit changes, so there's room for as much variety as your senior wants. We'll guide outfit choices ahead of the session.
Can we include dance, sports, music, or hobbies?
Yes, and we encourage it. A senior's interests can be woven into Signature and All-Inclusive sessions without making the day feel like a theme shoot.
Do parents help choose final images?
Yes. Parents are welcome in the gallery review process, and we're happy to talk through favorites together.
When do we receive the gallery?
Senior galleries are typically delivered within two to three weeks, with a few favorites shared sooner.
Do you offer prints or albums?
Yes. Personal printing rights are included with every collection, and albums or fine art prints can be added.
What is the difference between senior portraits and NP Seniors?
Senior portrait collections are our standard senior photography sessions. NP Seniors is a separate, limited program for a more connected experience, with seasonal opportunities and early access.
Is payment due at booking?
Senior sessions are reserved with payment in full at booking unless otherwise arranged.
