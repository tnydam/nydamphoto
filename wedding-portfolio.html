We approach every wedding with the same belief: your day deserves to be documented with honesty and heart. Not directed. Not staged. Just present, for the moments that matter most, from the quiet anticipation before you walk down the aisle to the last song of the night.

Arlen has been photographing weddings since 2007. He earned his PhD in English and started shooting at the same time, and the two were never separate. He's a storyteller who reads a room the way a close reader reads a page: looking for what's true, what's fleeting, and what will still matter twenty years from now.

Questions

Frequently
Asked

How far in advance should we book?+
Peak Austin dates (spring and fall Saturdays) book 12 to 18 months in advance. If your date is sooner, reach out anyway — we'll let you know availability immediately.
Do you travel outside Austin?+
Yes. We're based in Austin and available worldwide. Destination travel fees are discussed before booking. We love the Hill Country, the Texas coast, and beyond.
When will we receive our photos?+
Edited images are delivered to a private online gallery within 4 to 6 weeks of your wedding day. Fully edited, ready to download, share, and print.
Can we customize a collection?+
Absolutely. If none of the three collections feel exactly right, reach out and we'll put together something tailored to your day, your venue, and your vision.
Is the engagement session really complimentary?+
Yes, included in the Signature and Wedding Story collections. It's how you get comfortable in front of the camera so your wedding portraits feel natural, not forced.
