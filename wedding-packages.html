Our Approach
Every story is unique.
And deserves to be told.

We believe every wedding day is a story unlike any other, and it deserves to be documented with honesty and heart.

Our approach is rooted in storytelling — not directing, not interrupting, but being present for the moments that matter. From the quiet anticipation before you walk down the aisle to the last song of the night, we are there to capture every emotion as it naturally unfolds.

To make planning simple, we offer three wedding photography collections, thoughtfully designed to fit different wedding days and levels of investment.

Questions

Frequently
Asked

How far in advance should we book? +
Popular Austin wedding dates — especially spring and fall weekends — book 12–18 months in advance. If your date is sooner, reach out anyway. We'll let you know our availability and make it work if we can.
Do you travel outside Austin? +
Yes — we're based in Austin and available worldwide. Destination wedding travel fees are discussed and agreed upon before booking. We love shooting across the Hill Country, the Texas coast, and beyond.
When will we receive our photos? +
Within 48 hours of your wedding day, you'll receive a sneak peek of 10 to 15 images so you can start sharing the joy right away. Your full gallery of favorites, fully processed and retouched, is delivered within 4 to 6 weeks. From your gallery you can download, share, and order prints directly. Your selected album images and wall art prints are prepared from your favorites once you've had a chance to look through everything.
Can we customize a collection? +
Absolutely. Every wedding day is different. If none of the three collections feel exactly right, reach out and we'll put together something tailored to your day, your venue, and your vision.
What is your payment structure? +
A non-refundable retainer of $1,082.50 ($1,000 + tax) is due at signing to hold your date. The remaining balance is due 5 days before your wedding. We accept all major payment methods.
Is the engagement session really complimentary? +
Yes, included in the Signature and Wedding Story collections. The engagement session matters — it's how you get comfortable in front of the camera so your wedding portraits feel natural, not forced.
